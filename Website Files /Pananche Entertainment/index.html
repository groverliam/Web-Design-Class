About us

Panache Entertainment is the brainchild of Investment Banker turned Event Organizer/Fashion Show Choreographer Nishi Bahl. Panache Entertainment is the one stop shop to organizing, hosting and managing events from the inception, concept, technicals, Entertainment, to hosting celebrities! Uniting the East to the West, Panache Entertainment intends to revolutionize the Indian culture with the perfect blend to the West. Prior to internet, “Google” and cell phones and other vast technology, Nishi Bahl has brought India to the US much closer than ever before. An accomplished investment Banker, Nishi Bahl has been involved in the entertainment and cultural industry since 1987. Be it a bollywood celebrity, or volunteer charity work, she has never stepped back in supporting and promoting Indian Culture in the US. Choreographing ramp walks for the pioneering Miss India America pageants, Miss India USA pageants to the original Natraj Awards and multiple other bollywood shows.

Panache Entertainment specializes to be a fashion show company, hosting various fashion shows at high scale fashion show ramps nationwide. Panache hails individuals and experience whom coordinate various fashion shows and organized events for charitable organizations such as AAHOA, IIFA Galas, AAPI National conventions, and Heart and Hand for the handicapped.
